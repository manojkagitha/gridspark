### Absolute Claims to Revise

1. **"100% on-time delivery"**

   - **Fix:** "99%+ on-time delivery record" or "100% on-time delivery in 2024*"

   - **Issue:** Could be interpreted as performance guarantee
   - **Fix:** "Track Measurable Results" or "ROI Transparency"

   - **Issue:** Superlative claim requires substantiation
   - **Fix:** "Industry-leading" or "Award-winning"
